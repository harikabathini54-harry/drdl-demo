PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE CHECK LIST / OBSERVATION SHEET
MECHANICAL: CHECK STATUS REMARKS
SL. NO. ACTIVITY OK NOT OK
1.0LUBRICATION SYSTEM
1.1Check lubrication system for proper functioning
1.2Clean / Replace the filter
1.3Top up oil level in lube tank
1.4Check Lube Time and Duration
1.5Check the oil leakage & prevent them, if noticed
2.0SPINDLE
2.1Lubricate the spindle with recommended lubricants
2.2Check the spindle for its free movement
3.0AXES & SLIDES
3.1Check the Axis movement and Check the Axis noise in rapid movement.
3.2Check Slide Seals, Inspect covers/Guards & wipers
3.3Ensure Lubrication on Slides / Ball Screw
3.4Clean all Gibs and Slides for smooth operation
3.5Check Coupling, Belt Condition & Tension
4.0TRANSMISSION SYSTEM FOR CNC MACHINES
4.1Check the belt tension condition, adjust or replace accordingly
4.2Observe noise / missing transmission at couplings, Gearing (wherever applicable /rectify if required)
4.3Check the dust wipers on either side of the ball or lead screw
5.0HYDRAULIC & PNEUMATIC SYSTEM
5.1Clean / Replace the filters
5.2Check the oil level and its condition replace or top up to the specified level
5.3Check the pressure for its proper setting and adjust if necessary
5.4Check for proper functioning of pressure gauges
5.5Prevent all leakages (air / oil)
